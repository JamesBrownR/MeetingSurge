Common Questions

Do I need to provide content and photos?

I'll work with whatever you have. If you have photos of your shop, team, and work, great. If not, I can guide you on what to shoot with your phone. For written content, I can write it based on our conversation about your services.

Can you help with the domain and hosting?

Yes. I can help you choose and register a domain, set up hosting, and handle all the technical setup. Or if you already have a domain and hosting, I can work with what you have.

What if I need changes after launch?

Minor tweaks within the first 30 days are included. After that, I offer maintenance packages or you can request changes as needed for a small fee.

Will I be able to update the site myself?

For simple text changes, yes. I can set you up with an easy way to edit content. For design changes or adding new pages, I handle it for you to maintain quality.

Do you build sites for shops outside South Florida?

Yes. While I'm based in South Florida and most of my clients are local, I work with auto shops across the country remotely.

What about SEO?

Basic on-page SEO is included. Proper page structure, meta tags, mobile optimization, fast loading speeds. For ongoing SEO and ranking improvement, I offer that as a separate service.
