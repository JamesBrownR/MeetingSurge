What's Built Into Every Site

Custom Code, No Templates

Every line is written specifically for your shop. No WordPress, no Wix, no page builders. Custom code loads faster, ranks better, and doesn't look like every other auto shop site on the internet.

Mobile-First Design

Over 76% of people searching for auto repair are on their phones. Your site is designed for mobile first, then scaled up to desktop. It works perfectly on every screen size, every time.

Fast Load Times

If your site takes more than 3 seconds to load, most people leave before they see anything. I optimize every image, script, and line of code so your site loads quickly, which also helps you rank higher on Google.

Local SEO Built In

Your city, your services, and your keywords are woven throughout the site from day one. Proper title tags, meta descriptions, schema markup, and heading structure so Google knows exactly who you are and where you are.

Individual Service Pages

One generic "Services" page does not rank. You get a dedicated page for each service you offer, each one optimized for the keywords customers in your area are actually searching. More pages means more chances to show up.

Click-to-Call on Every Page

A customer on their phone should be able to call you in one tap from any page on your site. Every page has a prominent, working click-to-call button. No hunting around for a phone number.

Photo Integration

Real photos of your shop, your team, and your work build trust faster than anything else. I place them where they do the most good and make sure they're optimized so they don't slow the site down.

Google Reviews on Your Site

Your Google reviews show up directly on your homepage. Customers who find you through search see your rating before they even call. Social proof that converts visitors who are still deciding.

Google Maps Integration

An embedded map, clear directions, and your address displayed prominently. Customers should never have trouble finding you. It also reinforces your location to Google, which helps with local rankings.

Why Generic Sites Don't Work for South Florida Shops

South Florida is a dense, competitive market. In Broward County alone there are hundreds of auto repair shops. When someone in Plantation searches "mechanic near me," Google has plenty of options to choose from. The sites that rank are the ones built for local SEO from the ground up, not Wix templates with a logo swapped in.

A site built on a template has generic title tags, one catch-all services page, no schema markup, and no local keywords. Google can't tell what you do or where you do it. That's why shops with worse reputations and fewer reviews end up ranking above you.
