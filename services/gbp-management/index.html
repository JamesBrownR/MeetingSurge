What if I haven't claimed my profile yet?

No problem. I'll walk you through claiming and verifying it. Google usually sends a postcard with a verification code, which takes about 5-7 days. Once verified, I handle everything else.

Do you respond to negative reviews?

Yes. I respond to every review professionally. For negative reviews, I acknowledge the issue, apologize if warranted, and offer to make it right offline. This shows potential customers you handle problems well.
