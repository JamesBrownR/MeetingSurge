Common Questions

Will you need my Google account password?

No. You add me as a manager through your Google Business Profile settings. You stay the owner and can remove my access at any time. I never need your password.

What if I haven't claimed my profile yet?

No problem. I'll walk you through the claiming and verification process. Google usually sends a postcard with a verification code, which takes about 5 to 7 days. Once it's verified, I take over from there.

Do you write the review responses or do I approve them first?

Either way works. Most clients let me handle responses and just review the monthly report. If you want to approve each response before it goes up, we can set that up. Either way, responses go out within 24 hours.

How do you handle fake or spam reviews?

I flag them to Google right away and walk you through the dispute process. While waiting on Google's response, I write a professional public reply so potential customers can see the review is being addressed.

How long until I see results?

Your profile will look noticeably better within the first week. Ranking improvements on Google Maps typically show up within 30 to 60 days of consistent management, depending on how competitive your area is.

Do you work with shops outside South Florida?

Yes. Most of my clients are in Broward and Miami-Dade counties, but I manage profiles for shops across the country remotely. The work is the same regardless of location.
